World War I
The First World War was a worldwide battle fought by two alliances: the Allies and the Central Powers. There was fighting throughout Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East and the Pacific.
Who were the Allies and the Central Powers?

The Allies were: France, Great Britain, Italy, Japan, Russia, Serbia and the United States.

The Central Powers were: Austria-Hungary, Bulgaria, Germany and Turkey (Ottoman Empire).
